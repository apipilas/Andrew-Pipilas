HealthShare

HealthShare was my Engineering capstone project which was aimed to improve the communication, visibility, and efficiency of the patient to healthcare provider relationship. The goal was to take the existing Ontario health card and convert to an NFC capable object that would be used as a “check-in” tool as well as the primary form of log-in credentials into the HealthShare system. The idea was that a patient would tap their card to check-in to the doctor’s office for example, and any updates to their records, prescriptions and card activity could be updated dynamically through physician input and card activity statuses.

What I did: UI/UX design and deployment, backend communication, testing.

Tools: HTML, CSS, Node.js, Java, Git

Threaded

Threaded was a project I worked on to improve my web development toolset. It is a geolocation app that allows users to share landmarks and locations within Hamilton Ontario to create a user generated pool of pinned locations to explore. Users could create accounts to then use latitude/longitude coordinates to pin on google maps their image, description, and location of the landmark they wanted to share.

Tools: HTML, CSS, Javascript,php, Git, MySql, AWS(EC2), Amazon S3 buckets
